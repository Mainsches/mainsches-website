Privacy Policy

Mainsches AI – Sketch to Image

This Privacy Policy explains how Mainsches AI handles information when you use the mobile app “Mainsches AI – Sketch to Image”.

1. Who we are

Mainsches AI is provided by Mainsches.

Contact: support@mainsches.com

2. What the app does

The app allows users to enter a text prompt and optionally upload a sketch or photo. Based on this input, the app generates an AI-created image.

3. What data we process

  • Text prompts you enter in the app.
  • Images or sketches you choose to upload for generation.
  • Generated images returned to the app.
  • A device-related identifier used to help enforce free usage limits and reduce abuse.
  • Basic technical request data that may be processed by our hosting or infrastructure providers for service delivery and security.

4. How we use data

  • To generate AI images from your prompt and optional sketch.
  • To return generated results to your device.
  • To operate free usage limits and prevent abuse of the service.
  • To maintain app stability, reliability, and security.

5. Local storage on your device

Generated images and history entries may be stored locally on your device so they remain available after restarting the app. If you enable auto-save, generated images can also be saved to your device gallery.

We do not require an account to use the app.

6. Server-side processing

When you generate an image, your prompt and any uploaded sketch/image are sent to our backend and to third-party AI processing providers strictly for image generation functionality.

We do not sell your personal data.

7. Third-party services

The app may use infrastructure and AI service providers to process requests and deliver image generation functionality. These providers may process the submitted content only as needed to provide their services to us.

8. Data retention

We aim to keep server-side data retention minimal. Generated content that is stored locally in the app remains on your device unless you remove the app data, uninstall the app, or delete the content manually.

9. Children

This app is not directed to children.

10. Your choices

  • You can choose whether to upload an image or only use text.
  • You can delete the app and its local data at any time.
  • You can choose whether to enable auto-save for generated images.

11. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app and service data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@mainsches.com

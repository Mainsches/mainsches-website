Privacy notice

Privacy

This privacy notice explains how mainsches.com handles personal data when you visit the website or use the contact form.

Controller

Who is responsible

Mainsches
Contact: contact@mainsches.com
Country of operation: Switzerland

Website visits

Technical data

When you visit the website, technical information may be processed to deliver the website securely and reliably. This can include IP address, browser information, device type, operating system, referral information, access time and requested pages.

Contact form

Inquiry processing

If you use the contact form, the submitted details are processed to handle your inquiry and send a reply. This may include your name, email address, subject line, message content and technical metadata needed for abuse prevention and delivery.

Spam protection

Fraud and abuse prevention

The contact flow includes anti-spam checks such as hidden honeypot fields, form timing validation, request-rate limitation and technical request evaluation. This helps protect the website and communication channel from abuse.

Hosting and backend

Vercel and server processing

The website is hosted on Vercel. Contact requests are processed through server-side functions. Technical request data may therefore be processed by hosting and infrastructure providers to operate, secure and improve the service.

Analytics

Vercel Analytics

The website uses Vercel Analytics to understand general website usage and performance. The analytics setup is intended to provide aggregated insight into page views and site interaction patterns without unnecessary complexity.

Purpose and legal basis

Why data is processed

  • to provide and secure the website,
  • to respond to inquiries,
  • to prevent spam and misuse,
  • to understand overall website performance and usage.

Retention

How long data may be kept

Personal data is stored only as long as necessary for the relevant purpose, legal obligations, security needs or communication follow-up. Exact retention periods can vary depending on the type of inquiry and the technical context.

Your rights

Access and requests

You may request information about personal data relating to you, and you may ask for correction or deletion where applicable. For privacy-related requests, please contact: contact@mainsches.com

Updates

Changes to this notice

This privacy notice may be updated if the website, its features or legal requirements change.

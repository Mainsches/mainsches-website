Workflow

A simple premium flow from rough sketch to stronger visual direction.

Mainsches AI is designed to shorten the distance between an early concept and something that feels clearer, more tangible and easier to discuss. The workflow stays simple: add a sketch, describe the idea, generate the result.

Upload the sketch

Start with a rough drawing, a furniture concept, a product idea or any simple visual draft that captures the shape of the concept.

Describe it in chat

Add a short prompt to guide the output. The chat-based flow makes the process feel natural, direct and easy to iterate.

Generate the image

Turn the sketch into a more realistic visual that is stronger for feedback, presentation and next-step decision making.
